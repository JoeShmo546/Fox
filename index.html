The Story of the Quick Brown Fox

Once upon a time there was a quick brown fox. Every morning the quick brown fox would:

  • run through the forest,
  • swim through the pond,
  • and finish the day by gobbling one of Farmer Brown's chickens.

One day the quick brown fox, while on his daily run,

  1. jumped over a lazy doge
  2. tripped on a loge and
  3. fell into a lions mouth.

And the lion lived hapily ever after.

The End
